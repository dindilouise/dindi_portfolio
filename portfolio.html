The Bandana Bird Brigade


For this project, we were given the task of creating a story or game; my partner, Karina Gutierrez, and I decided
to create a story based game. The storyline of this game is about two birds PoPo and JuJu, who are part of an organization
that helps animals in need, they go about looking for animals in distress and the goal of the game is to help decide what the
birds are going to do. The process of creating this game was split into small tasks so it was easier to complete the whole
game and find the little bugs and glitches earlier. The game’s programming consists of many broadcast signals that would trigger
decisions markers to disappear or appear, as well as, make the birds talk and have different dialogues. We also nested several if-then
statements, which was a main portion in making the game functioning. A major problem we had was that there was a glitch during
the introduction of the storyline, the player was able to change characters mid way the intro part. We fixed this by making a list
and adding the bird characters to it, to make sure that this problem wouldn’t stay within the game. I am very please with the outcome
of the game, it is very aesthetic and interactive. To see for yourself, click the picture to play the game.




Image Artist

This is the original picture me and my partner decided to alter for this project.
The objective of this project was to alter a picture of our choosing in some type of way, so my partner and I picked
this picture and decided to alter the RGB colors. It was difficult at first to figure out how to get the images to
show up, but then after trial and error, my partner and I figured out that if we call the image to show it will pop up.
Also, we learned that changing the R B G in the section changes the colors into a certain color that isn't called upon.
We ended up creating 4 different styles of pictures, which are all not the same. It has different background colors,
different face colors; all creating different types of images. If we were to continue with this project and manipulating
the image's RBG code we could make art similar to the style of art pop. Understanding the process of this manipulation
allows me to understand the concept of other color image manipulation.

though it would be funny to get more followers, but it ended in a disaster. It made MySpace shut down for 2 hours and his profile was deleted after hitting
over a million followers. This occured in only the span of around 2 days. The federal government also got involved since it was the first type of hacking with

That malicous code could be latched onto anything and everyone. It can come in many forms that can seem harmless, but the effects of viruses can be extraordinary, to the point
the federal government becomes involved. The only way a person can be safe from a virus is by not using any electronics, because not even the

Data Analysis
